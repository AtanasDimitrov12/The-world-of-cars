Mercedes-Benz GT1000

A Masterpiece and a quintessential supercar capable of exploring the absolute maximum of what can be achieved through modern performance engineering - reaching cosmic heights and turning the seemingly impossible into reality. From shattered world top-speed records to an unmistakable signature look that has stood the test of time, fine-tuned by several generations – every BRABUS product carrying the Rocket badge represents the highest level. Each more powerful than the last, mastering every single frontier of design and performance in typical BRABUS fashion. Except for one. Where can we take it from here? Production will be limited to 25 units worldwide.

BMW M8 Competition

The BMW M8 epitomizes luxury and performance with its potent engines, sleek design, and advanced technology. Available in coupe, convertible, and gran coupe variations, it offers versatility to suit diverse preferences. The M8's powerful engine, often a twin-turbocharged V8, ensures exhilarating acceleration and dynamic driving experiences. Its aggressive styling cues, premium interiors, and cutting-edge technology create a refined and comfortable environment. Technological innovations, such as advanced infotainment systems and driver assistance features, enhance convenience and safety on the road. With precise handling and customizable driving modes, the BMW M8 delivers an engaging and dynamic driving experience, whether on the highway or winding mountain roads. As the pinnacle of luxury and performance in the BMW lineup, the M8 is a testament to BMW's commitment to excellence. Enthusiasts can explore the latest specifications and availability of the BMW M8 on BMW's official website or at local dealerships.
